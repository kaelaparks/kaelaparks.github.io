Leadership Roles in Associations

I have had the fortune to be involved in professional associations at both the regional and national level and have greatly enjoyed the honor and opportunity to help inform my field.

  • AHEAD Access to Technology Series is a series of professional development opportunities being offered online through the Association on Higher Education and Disability. As co-chair of the Standing Committee on Technology I have been responsible for working collaboratively to facilitate the cultivation and deployment of a webinar series as well as a conference strand.
  • ORAHEAD is the Association on Higher Education and Disability. I have been proud to serve on the Board of Directors, First as President-elect, then as President, and now as Past-President. In my tenure I have introudced pre-conference sessions and drafted and passed revisions to the by-laws.
