Reel Eyes

The Reel Eyes Showcase of Films By and About People With Disabilities ran for nine consecutive years in Alaska. Locations varied from year to year, as did the type of credit that could be earned, but all events were open to the community.

  • 2011 Lineup featured films in Anchorage, Mat-su, Soldotna, Valdez, and Homer. The Anchorage event was a four part series that was offered both live, and online and featured an opportunity for students to earn 400 level Social Work credit.
  • 2010 Lineup featured films in Homer, Anchorage, Valdez, Juneau, Soldotna, Fairbanks, Mat-Su, Nome, and Kotzebue. Credit was available at both the 100 level in Student Leadership and the 400 level in Social Work
  • 2009 Lineup featured showings in Anchoragae, Mat-Su, Valdez, and Solddotna with an option to earn 100 level Psychology credit
